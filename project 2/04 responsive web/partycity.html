Party City (2018)

A class assignment for my animation 3 class. I had to create a 15 second PSA, commercial, or TV show opening (I did a commercial for Party City because, at the time, it was getting close to Halloween). I used the app, Procreate to draw the images and then I compiled the finished images into AfterEffects to time it into 15 seconds.
